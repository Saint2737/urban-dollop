CapEx and OpEx

In the world of corporate finance and accounting, two critical terms come to play and take center stage; capital expenditure(CapEx) and operating expenditure(OpEx).

Businesses must plan their capital/revenue with regard to the optimal values of the two factors since CapEx and OpEx are critical in maintaining business operation and ensuring optimal performance of the business.

Capital Expenditure

Capital expenditure are long-term Investments meant to serve the business/company over multiple years.

They can be in form of assets and acquisition intended for business growth and expansion. They can also be in the form of company savings.

CapEx comprises:

-Growth and Expansion

-Savings

Operating expenditure

Operating expenditure represent the ongoing day to day essential costs associated with the running and operation of the business.

Operating expenditure is at the heart of business operations. It is allocated to the operating expenses or costs of the business.
