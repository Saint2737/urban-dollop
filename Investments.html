Payback Period

PBP indicates the duration required by a project to recover the initial investment through cumulative cash flows. Additionally, managers utilize PBP to determine the projected duration for a project to recoup its investment. Generally, projects with a shorter PBP are deemed acceptable for a company. It fundamentally gauges the duration required to reach the break-even point.

Accounting Rate of Return

The Accounting Rate of Return (ARR) is a financial measure that evaluates the profitability of an investment by comparing the average annual accounting profit produced by the investment to its initial cost, presented as a percentage. It's a straightforward and comprehensible approach for assessing investment projects.
